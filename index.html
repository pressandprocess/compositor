Compositor manages AI conversation memory across platforms. Drop session summaries here and the parser assembles them into a structured repository you own. Generate briefing packs to carry context into every new session.
Step 01
Get a summary
At the end of any AI session, ask for a Markdown summary. The recommended prompt is in Settings.
Step 02
Drop or paste
Tag the session and drop the file or paste the text. The parser extracts decisions, threads, constraints.
Step 03
Compose the brief
Open Repository, filter by topic and date, copy the assembled briefing into your next AI session.
